My Story

Japan has been a place of constant discovery for me. From early mornings on quiet platforms to hidden neighborhood cafés, every trip taught me something new.

Over time, I realized that small details—like which seat to book or when to visit a neighborhood—can transform a confusing trip into a great one.

fujiseat.com is the result of countless journeys, notes, and conversations with locals and fellow travelers.
